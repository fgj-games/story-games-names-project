MURDERERS AND VICTIMS, OKLAHOMA TERRITORY, 1875-1895
Compiled by Jason Morningstar

From the records of Isaac Parker, Judge for the U.S. District Court for the Western District of Arkansas during that period. Presiding from Fort Smith, Parker’s jurisdiction included Indian Territory, present-day Oklahoma. The Indian names below would represent Choctaw, Chickasaw, Cherokee, Seminole and Creek.
