TLINGIT
Compiled by Jason Morningstar
NOTES
Tlingit society is divided into two moeities - Raven and Eagle/Wolf - with complex kinship rules.  Within each moiety are clans to which individuals are attached in family houses.  My apologies to any Tlingit who might be reading this - I've made a hash of your fascinating culture.
