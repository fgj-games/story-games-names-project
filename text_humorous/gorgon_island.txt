GORGON HUNTERS OF GORGON ISLAND
Compiled by Team Story-Games 
NOTES
These names were the result of an informal contest to name the iconic characters appearing with the warrior woman Fyren Cloudfloater in the Drowning and Falling Role-playing Game.  The winners were Gwarl Ovensinger (Dwarfling), Mabel Firewind (Wizard), Silverblade Spellweaver (Elf), and, of course, Jaesyn the Mørnyngstär (Cleric).
